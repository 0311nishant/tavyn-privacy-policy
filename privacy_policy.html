Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

üîí Privacy First Promise

Your data stays on your device. Tavyn operates 100% locally and does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal information with us or third parties.

1. Introduction

Tavyn ("we," "our," or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Tavyn handles your data when you use our Chrome extension.

Key Principle: All data processing happens locally on your device. We do not operate any servers, databases, or analytics systems that collect your information.

2. Information We Access

To provide tab management and session saving functionality, Tavyn accesses the following information locally on your device only:

2.1 Web Data

  • Tab URLs: Website addresses of your open tabs
  • Tab Titles: Page titles of your open tabs
  • Timestamps: When tabs were opened or sessions were saved
  • Tab State: Whether tabs are pinned, muted, or active

2.2 Website Content

  • Page Text: When you use the search feature with "search in content" enabled, Tavyn extracts text from web pages to enable searching within page content
  • Favicons: Website icons for visual identification

2.3 User Preferences

  • Saved Sessions: Tab collections you choose to save
  • Session Notes: Optional notes you add to saved sessions
  • Collapsed Tabs: Tabs you temporarily close using the extension

3. How We Use Your Information

All data accessed by Tavyn is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  1. Duplicate Detection: Identifying tabs from the same domain to help you manage duplicates
  2. Session Management: Saving and restoring your browsing sessions
  3. Universal Search: Searching across your open tabs, collapsed tabs, and saved sessions
  4. Crash Recovery: Auto-saving your tabs to recover from unexpected browser closures
  5. Tab Organization: Temporarily storing collapsed tabs for later restoration

4. Data Storage

4.1 Local Storage Only

All data collected by Tavyn is stored using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API, which stores data locally on your device. This data never leaves your computer.

4.2 What Is Stored

  • Saved browsing sessions
  • Collapsed tab information
  • Auto-save backups (cached every 10 seconds)
  • Crash recovery data

4.3 Cache Duration

Page content extracted for search purposes is cached for 2 minutes to improve performance. This cache is stored in memory and cleared automatically.

5. Data Sharing and Transmission

‚úÖ Zero Data Transmission

Tavyn does NOT:

  • Send any data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Share data with third parties
  • Collect personally identifiable information
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Require account creation or login
  • Communicate with any remote services

6. Permissions Explanation

Tavyn requests the following Chrome permissions for functionality:

6.1 Required Permissions

  • tabs: Required to detect duplicate tabs, manage tab collapsing/restoring, and organize your open tabs.
  • storage: Stores saved sessions, collapsed tabs, and auto-save data locally on your device.
  • scripting: Injects content scripts to extract page text for the search-within-content feature.
  • activeTab: Identifies the current active tab to provide context-aware tab management.

6.2 Host Permissions (<all_urls>)

This permission allows Tavyn to inject content scripts on websites for the "search in content" feature. The extension only accesses page content when you explicitly use the search feature, and the content is never transmitted outside your browser.

7. Your Rights and Control

7.1 Data Access

You can view all data stored by Tavyn at any time:

  1. Open Chrome DevTools (F12)
  2. Go to "Application" ‚Üí "Storage" ‚Üí "Extension Storage"
  3. Select Tavyn to view all stored data

7.2 Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data and can delete it anytime:

  • Clear All Data: Uninstall the extension from chrome://extensions/
  • Clear Specific Data: Delete individual sessions, collapsed tabs, or auto-saves from within the extension
  • Clear Cache: The search cache is automatically cleared when you close Chrome

7.3 Opt-Out of Features

  • Search in Content: Uncheck "Search page content" to prevent page text extraction
  • Auto-Save: The extension auto-saves tabs for crash recovery. To disable this, you would need to uninstall the extension

8. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API:

  • Data is protected by your operating system's security
  • Data is only accessible when you're logged into your Chrome profile
  • No data transmission means no risk of interception
  • No external servers means no risk of server breaches

9. Third-Party Services

Tavyn does not use any third-party services, libraries, or APIs. The extension is entirely self-contained and operates offline.

10. Children's Privacy

Tavyn does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The extension does not collect any personal information from any users, regardless of age.
